    What is your

    Skin Type?

    Skin Type Test

    Don't know your skin type? Follow these easy steps to determine if you have a normal, dry, oily, or combination skin type!
    1. Wash your face with lukewarm water.
    2. Wait an hour.
    3. Time to look at your skin!

    What to Look For:

    Normal Skin Type: Your face will appear smooth with no dry or oily/shiny patches.
    Dry Skin Type: Your face will feel tight and have dry patches.
    Oily Skin Type: Your face will appear shinier and have larger pores.
    Combination Skin Type: Your face will appear shinier on your forehead and nose while having normal-to-dry skin everywhere else.
